## Inspectlet

[**Inspectlet**](https://www.inspectlet.com) is a session recording, heatmap, form analytics, and AI session insights platform for product and growth teams. Used by thousands of websites since 2011.

A modern alternative to LogRocket, FullStory, and Hotjar — with deep form analytics, AI-powered session insights, and a free tier that's free forever.
